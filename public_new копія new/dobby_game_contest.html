DOBBY GAME COVER ART CONTEST

📅 August 26, 2025 ⏱️ 2 min read New Contest
🎮🎨

We are back with another epic community challenge this time, we're diving into the world of gaming! It's time for the DOBBY GAME COVER ART CONTEST your chance to imagine what a Dobby video game cover would look like.

Guidelines:

  • Theme: "Dobby Game Cover Art"
  • Open to all formats: digital, traditional, or experimental.
  • Imagine Dobby in any setting: futuristic adventure, cozy RPG world, or fast-paced action universe and bring your vision to life!
  • Keep it original

Submit Your Art

  1. Post on X (Twitter) with hashtags #SentientAGI and #DobbyGamer
  2. Include your Discord username on your X post
  3. Drop your entry in the ⁠🎨│artist-junior or ⁠🎨│artist-senior on our Discord
  4. Mention "Art Contest Submission" when you post your work on Discord

📅 Submission Deadline

September 2, 2025 at 7:59 PM

🎮 Rewards & Recognition:

  • Additional points for higher AGI roles
  • Special mentions and spotlights for outstanding entries
  • It's game time with your art!
